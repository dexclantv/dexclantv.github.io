How to export YouTube cookies (fast)

  1. On your computer, open Chrome (or Firefox) and sign in at youtube.com.
  2. Install a cookies exporter:
    • Chrome: Get cookies.txt
    • Firefox: cookies.txt
  3. While on youtube.com, open the extension and click Export. Copy the text.
  4. Return here, check “Use my YouTube cookies”, paste into the box, hit Save, then Generate Clips.

Tip: Cookies expire. If it stops working later, export again.
