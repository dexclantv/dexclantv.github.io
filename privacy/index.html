Privacy Protocol

LAST_UPDATED: JUNE 12, 2025
STATUS: ACTIVE

01. Data Collection

We minimize data intake to essential vectors only. The system collects:

  • Account Identifiers: Google/Apple email and profile images for authentication.
  • Platform Data: YouTube analytics metrics (retention, views) only when explicitly authorized via OAuth.
  • AI Interactions: Input prompts and output generations for scripts and titles. These are processed locally or ephemerally and not used for model training without consent.
  • Payment Info: Managed entirely by Apple/StoreKit. We do not store credit card integers.
02. Usage Parameters

Collected data is utilized strictly to:

  • Power visual retention graphs and CTR alert systems.
  • Personalize the dashboard interface.
  • Refine AI generation context.
  • Validate subscription clearance levels.
03. Storage & Security

DexTracker operates on a "Local-First" philosophy.

  • Data resides primarily on your local device.
  • Manual export protocols are available.
  • Cloud synchronization occurs only upon user activation.
04. User Rights

You maintain full command authority over your data:

  • Deletion: Removing the app purges local caches. Account deletion requests wipe server-side references.
  • Revocation: Access to YouTube data can be revoked via your Google Account Security settings.
05. Age Restrictions

DexTracker is not designed for operatives under 13 years of age. No data is knowingly harvested from minors.
