FAQ

What is DexTracker?
DexTracker is an AI-powered assistant that helps you grow your YouTube channel by turning retention analytics into clear insights, clips, and better titles — all from your phone.
How does auto-clipping work?
We detect the strongest parts of your video based on watch time and retention, then let you export those moments as vertical clips ready for Shorts, TikTok, and Reels.
Is there a free trial?
Yes. The iOS app includes a 7-day free trial so you can test auto-clipping, retention tools, and SEO features before upgrading.
